A bit about me

Hello, I'm Maurice, I'm a Mechatronics and Embedded Systems engineer from Queensland, Australia. Specializing in robotics engineering.

My work spans across a vast array of robotics and firmware related areas, including developing SLAM algorithms for drone navigation, designing systems from the ground up, and delving into firmware reverse engineering.

My technical proficiency includes Python, Embedded C, and GoLang, alongside tools like Atmel Studio 7 and J-Link, contributing to robust solutions in the robotics and mechatronics landscape.
